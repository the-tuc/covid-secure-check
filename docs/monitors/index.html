Become a workplace monitor

We’re asking people to take a short survey about how safe their workplace is. This will help us measure how safe different jobs are during the pandemic, as people go back to work.

We can’t help resolve your problems directly, or offer individual advice, but we need to know what’s going on so we have an accurate picture of how employers are responding to health and safety concerns. This will help us put pressure on all employers to live up to the best standards.

We’ll never publish individual survey responses.

Enter your email address to become a workplace monitor

We’ll use your email to send you a survey every couple of weeks. You'll get your first one as soon as you sign up.
