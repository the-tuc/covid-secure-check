alpha This is a new service from the TUC — your feedback will help us to improve it.

COVID Secure Check collects the Covid-19 risk assessments published by employers and monitors good and bad practices in different jobs. The evidence we gather will be used to try and improve everyone’s working conditions during the pandemic.

! Warning If you’re worried about a safety issue where you work, read our guide to reporting it.
